ShelterSync

Foster care made simple and accountable — a single source of truth for animal shelters.

The Problem

Shelters Are Flying Blind

⚠️
Thousands lost in missing supplies Crates, medications, and food go out to foster families and never come back — with no paper trail.
🏥
Medical records fall through the cracks Foster families miss medication doses because no one communicated the animal's care plan properly.
⚖️
Liability risk is real Animals returned sicker than when they left, with no documentation — putting the entire shelter at legal risk.
The Solution

One Platform. Full Accountability.

🖥️
Admin Dashboard
Shelter staff manage all animals, track every item leaving the building, and maintain timestamped medical records.
🏠
Foster Portal
Foster families see exactly which animals they're caring for, supplies to return, and complete care instructions.
📦
Inventory Tracking
Real-time loan tracking ensures every crate, medication, and supply is accounted for from checkout to return.
🩺
Medical Logs
Full medical history per animal with timestamps and accountability — nothing is ever assumed or forgotten.
